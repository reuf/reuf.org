About the author of this page

My interest in cybersecurity dates back to my high school days over 20 years ago. It began with a fascination for trojans, keyloggers, and spyware. Although I later moved into web development, I have remained a lifelong learner and continue to follow and experiment with various security tools as a hobby. It's intriguing to see how IT security has evolved and become a vital tool for criminals, governments, and various teams worldwide.

In addition, I've been exploring data analytics, using tools such as SQL, Python, and R, and advanced Excel functionality for data ingestion, transformation, and exploration, as well as visualizing data with PowerBI and Tableau. My ability to deliver IT solutions stems from years of hands-on experience in both web development and data analytics, allowing me to craft efficient, secure, and scalable systems tailored to specific needs. Whether it's troubleshooting existing platforms or building from scratch, I excel at finding innovative solutions to complex technical challenges.

Beyond IT, I have a passion for teaching. I take pride in mentoring others in coding and mathematics, breaking down complex concepts into digestible and engaging lessons. Whether working with beginners or more advanced learners, my approach is rooted in patience, adaptability, and a genuine desire to foster understanding and confidence in technical subjects. Over the years, I’ve successfully guided students to overcome hurdles and achieve their learning goals, making the learning process enjoyable and productive.

When taking on projects, I cooperate with a wide range of acquaintances who are all highly skilled and experts in their respective fields. Their expertise brings immense value to the overall success of delivering IT solutions or enhancing the teachability of those who seek guidance. This collaborative approach ensures that every project benefits from diverse perspectives, innovative ideas, and exceptional execution.

I was born in Bosnia and have had the privilege of gaining a wide range of experiences by studying, working, and traveling worldwide. This global perspective has enriched my understanding of different cultures, work environments, and problem-solving approaches. My exposure to diverse industries and practices has further honed my adaptability, communication skills, and ability to work collaboratively with teams across the globe. It is this unique blend of technical expertise, teaching ability, and international experience that drives my success in delivering value in every endeavor.
